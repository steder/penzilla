What is Rock Paper Scissors Spock Lizard?

    RPSSL is just like Rock Paper Scissor(Roshambo). The only
    difference is the addition of Spock and a Lizard.

How does one play RPSSL?

    From www.samkass.com:
    Scissors cuts Paper covers Rock crushes Lizard poisons Spock smashes
    Scissors decapitates Lizard eats Paper disproves Spock vaporizes Rock
    crushes Scissors.
